Restaurants and Food

Most of the food supply is imported into Lobitos and Piedritas. Artisanal fishing is an essential component of the local economy

 

In Lobitos and Piedritas there are many local restaurants which offer a wide range of food including traditional seafood meals and much more

 

Recommended restaurants in Lobitos:

  • El Tranqui
  • El Ancla
  • Lobitos Point
  • Sabor Peruano de Cora
