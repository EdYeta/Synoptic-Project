TRANSPORTATION

Lobitos - Talaras

 

➼

Bicycle trip - Cycle along the Panamericana Norte road from Lobitos to Piedritas estimate 8.3km and take a break at restaurants [Villa Jordan/ Linda Finca/ Sol Y Campo]. Continue the journey to Talaras which around 4.4km. ( Bicycle provide by Hotel Buenavista Lobitos) Visit Buena Vista Lobitos Facebook page!

➼

Walking trip - Walk around 2 hours along the Panamericana Norte road from Lobitos to Piedritas. Continue the journey around 1 hour to reach Talaras.

➼

General Taxi - Costing around S/30-50 from Talaras.

➼

Car rental - Reserve a car at Talaras airport, Bus station or other location online before travel to Talaras. Visit PERUVIAN RENT A CAR!
